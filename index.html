About Me

My professional and personal goals seems to blend together. I've always believed integrity and empathy should be pervasive in all that I do. On top of this, I'm accountable and continually drive work towards completion. I used to work for an international consulting firm performing automation work for complex data migrations, or financial data processing. I did very well but not spectacular. Designing an On-Demand Transit App for Public Transportation

Super proud of my first major full development of a web application. After tons of late nights, we developed a pilot product in use a some major cities in the US. The application is based in Dash Plotly, a python derivative of react.js. With minimal help, I designed the entire app. From the UI colors to the security controls on the backend APIs. THis product incorporates our Machine Learning optimization models into the scheduling of on-demand trips. The app also provides geolocation tracking of vehicles, ad-hoc optimization or ad-hoc manual changes to a fleet of vehicle's daily trip schedule.
